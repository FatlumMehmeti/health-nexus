# Roles

## SUPER_ADMIN
- manages global platform settings
- manages tenants and pricing strategy
- manages feature flags by plan and tenant

## TENANT_MANAGER
- manages tenant landing content
- manages doctors, services, products, plans, and contracts

## DOCTOR
- receives appointments
- completes appointment workflows

## SALES
- supports recruitment and guided consultation workflows

## CLIENT
- views tenant landing pages
- registers, enrolls, and books appointments
- purchases eligible plans and OTC products when enabled
